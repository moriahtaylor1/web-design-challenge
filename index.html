SUMMARY: TUMOR DIAGNOSIS VS. X


The purpose of this project was to analyze whether the diagnosis of tumors was related to its size or shape. To accomplish this analysis, two datasets from Kaggle.com were used - one representing benign tumors and the other representing malignant tumors.


R was used to build a series of density plots to showcase the relationships between diagnosis and different aspects of shape and size of tumors. Relationships analyzed included:
    - Diagnosis vs. Area                 - Diagnosis vs. Radius
    - Diagnosis vs. Compactness    - Diagnosis vs. Symmetry
    - Diagnosis vs. Concavity         - Diagnosis vs. Texture
